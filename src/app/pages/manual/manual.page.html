INSTRUCTIONS

Fizzy Buzz will challenge your mind, your fingers and the ability to count from 1 upwards!

You'll start with the number 1. Every two seconds, the number will increment by one.

Every time the number is divisible by 3, you'll have to hit the FIZZ button.

Every time the number is divisible by 5, you'll have to hit the BUZZ button.

Can the number be divided by BOTH 3 and 5? Then hit the FIZZBUZZ button!

Guess correctly as often as you can! If you guess incorrectly 3 times, it's game over!

Good Luck!
